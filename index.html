Regional MSW Annually



The above bar chart represents the Total Municipal Solid Waste (MSW) generated yearly in each region. MSW refers to the waste generated by householads and organisations within a municipal area.
The regional groupings are used by the United Nations and include:
East Asia Summit (EAS),
Eastern and Central Europe (ECS),
Latin America and the Caribbean (LCN),
Middle East and North Africa (MEA),
North America (NAC), Southeast Asia (SES),
